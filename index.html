What I Do

Wireframing

The first step of the UI design process is wireframing, after the design requirements are collected. I prefer paper-wireframing before turning it to a digital mockup.

Mockups

Mockups reflect the final appearance of the design. It makes us clear what the final product looks like. It helps the designers like me to take the reviews from the clients and improve them beforehand.

Interactive prototypes

Prototypes help to deliver the feel of the final product before stepping to development process. The usability of the final product can be implimented on prototypes.

Frontend Deveolpment

Converting Mockups and Prototypes to codes is another interesting part of the frontend deveolpment. One can get into a different dimention of fun writing the codes on the editors.
